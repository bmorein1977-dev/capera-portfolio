1. Developer

Purpose: Technical architect and maintainer of the system.

Responsibilities:

Build, configure, and update the database/platform.

Implement features (assessment uploads, reporting, workflows).

Fix bugs, ensure system security, and integrate with HR/LMS systems.

Support Super Admins with advanced system configurations.

2. Super Admin

Purpose: Owner of the system at the organizational level.

Responsibilities:

Create/manage Admin accounts.

Define system-wide permissions, workflows, and access rules.

Control the competency framework (e.g., elements, standards, assessment types).

Oversee reporting dashboards and compliance tracking.

Perform high-level audits of activity logs and user permissions.

3. Admin

Purpose: Day-to-day system operator for a business unit/site.

Responsibilities:

Enrol candidates, assign roles, and manage user access.

Assign competence standards/assessments to individuals.

Track progress and generate reports for managers and regulators.

Upload training records and link evidence to competence units.

Provide first-line support to Assessors and Candidates.

4. Internal Verifier (IV)

Purpose: Quality assurer of competence assessments.

Responsibilities:

Review assessment decisions made by Assessors.

Ensure assessments are consistent, fair, and in line with standards.

Sample candidate portfolios to check validity, reliability, and sufficiency.

Give feedback to Assessors on quality of evidence and judgment.

Sign off verified competence units for compliance purposes.

5. Competence Assessor

Purpose: Judge whether a Candidate meets the competence standard.

Responsibilities:

Conduct workplace assessments and knowledge checks.

Record evidence (observations, documents, witness testimonies).

Decide if competence is “met” or “not yet met.”

Provide constructive feedback to Candidates.

Upload assessment results into the database.

6. Candidate

Purpose: Person being assessed for competence.

Responsibilities:

Complete assigned assessments and training modules.

Upload or present evidence (workplace logs, documents, photos, videos).

Respond to knowledge questions and demonstrate tasks in practice.

Track own progress in the system.

Engage with Assessor and follow feedback.

7. Trainee

Purpose: Learner not yet in competence assessment stage (pre-competence).

Responsibilities:

Complete training courses, e-learning, and induction modules.

Begin collecting evidence for future competence assessments.

Access learning materials and practice tasks.

Transition into Candidate role once eligible for assessment.

✅ Hierarchy in Practice

Developer (technical build & maintenance)

Super Admin (system owner, full control)

Admin (daily operations, user management)

Internal Verifier (assurance & compliance)

Competence Assessor (assessment & feedback)

Candidate (seeking competence recognition)

Trainee (pre-competence learning stage)
